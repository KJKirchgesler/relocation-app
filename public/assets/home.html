What is Voyager

Voyager is a place to discover unique, cultural experiences from design, photography, street art experiences, food tours, dinners posted by locals, backroads, wine tastings, history, sightseeing and immersive cultural exchange. Voyager is a home for anyone looking to experience honest, local culture through experiences created and posted by passionate road trippers.

Put some road behind you, look forward to new experiences
About Us

Our team is composed of brilliant, driven people with an authentic passion for travel and a committment to bridging cultures. Collectively, we've traveled all over the country. Together, we strive to enable other voyagers, cultural exchange, community-building and exploration worldwide by empowering people to share their experiences with others. We believe that by exploring the world, we can make it a better place
